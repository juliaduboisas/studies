My Favorite Poems

Since I was a very lonely child, I have been reading for basically my whole life. Because of this, I have developed preferences on my favorite poems and poets. Currently, I have a PDF that includes about 120 pages of my favorite poetry, but that would be too long a website to make. Here are some of my favorite poems instead, with a small quote from each:

  • The truth the dead know

    by Anne Sexton


    It is June. I am tired of being brave.

  • The Orange

    by Wendy Cope


    I love you. I'm glad I exist.

  • Untitled (Blue, Green, & Brown): Oil on canvas: Mark Rothko: 1952

    by Ocean Vuong


    There is so much I want to tell you - but I only earned one life. & I took nothing.
